The closest thing you can compare Anime to would be Cartoons, however if you think that they are the same in every way, you would be very suprised. The main difference is the art, and the intended audiance. The art's biggest feature is the fact that characters have proportionally big eyes. The targeted audiance for most Anime is young adults, around the ages of 16 and onwards.
